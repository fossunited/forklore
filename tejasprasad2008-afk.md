### A small brief about your project

### One FOSS maintainer lesson for your younger self

Ship imperfect things. Waiting for perfection means nobody gets to use or improve your work. It's open source for a reason. It may not gain the traction you imagined in a month or even a year, but your commitment to improving it shows how reliable it is.

### Why do you do it? Why do you bother maintaining a FOSS project?

Because knowledge compounds when shared. Open source lets ideas outlive individuals, and seeing others build on your work is rewarding in ways metrics alone cannot capture.
